- Aqua lenses are a type of contact lens designed to provide a comfortable and natural feeling to the wearer. These lenses are made from a highly breathable silicone hydrogel material that allows for a high level of oxygen flow to the eye, reducing dryness and irritation.

-Aqua lenses are also designed to retain moisture, which helps to keep the eyes hydrated and comfortable. They are often recommended for people who experience dry eye syndrome or who work in environments with low humidity.

-here are different types of aqua lenses available, including daily disposable, weekly disposable, and monthly disposable options. Daily disposable lenses are designed to be worn once and then discarded, while weekly and monthly lenses are designed to be worn for longer periods of time before needing to be replaced.
